Creating a New Disk Partition

Follow these steps to create a new disk partition:

Step Description
1 Open Disk Management (Windows) or Disk Utility (Mac).
2 Select the disk or volume where you want to create the new partition.
3 Right-click (Windows) or Control-click (Mac) on the disk or volume and choose 'Shrink Volume' or 'Partition'.
4 Specify the size of the new partition and follow the prompts to complete the process.
